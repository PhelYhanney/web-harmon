Our Story

A Legacy Built in the Forest

Harmon World, LLC is an experienced and dedicated logging company servicing clients throughout Missouri. We offer each of our clients high-quality, professional logging services that are individualized to fit their specific logging and forestry needs.

Our family-owned logging company has been passed down for generations. We are passionate about providing you with nothing less than exceptional timber cutting services — backed by decades of hands-on experience and a deep respect for the land we work.

Whether you need land cleared, hazardous trees removed, forest health improved, or quality timber harvested, our team brings the skill, equipment, and dedication to get the job done right the first time.

Meet Our Director

Daniel Turner

Director — Harmon World, LLC

Daniel Turner is the driving force behind Harmon World, LLC. A certified Missouri Master Logger recognized by the Missouri Logging Council, Daniel brings decades of firsthand experience in timber harvesting, forest management, and land stewardship to every project he oversees.

Under Daniel's leadership, Harmon World, LLC has built a reputation for reliability, professionalism, and an unwavering commitment to quality. He personally ensures that each client receives a service tailored to their land's specific needs — from the initial evaluation to the final cleanup.

As a proud member of the American Loggers Council, Daniel stays current with industry best practices and safety standards, including maintaining full NFPA certification to protect both clients and crew on every job site.
